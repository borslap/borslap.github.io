Inverted Pendulum Nonlinear Control

Coursework November 2022

One of the most interesting classes I have taken so far was the advanced control systems. As a part of the course assessment I was tasked, with a team of peers to design and implement a control system that swings up and balances a gyroscope mounted pendulum using the precession torque.

Simulink Implementation of the Control System

During swing up, in which the system is conditionally stable, a Lyapunov control function is implemented to accelerate the pendulum. When the pendulum reaches the top position, state-space control transition is triggered during which the system is controlled based on a linear approximation.

Generative Design

Personal Project September 2022

I learned about generative design algorithms in one of my courses on additive manufacturing. Generative design is essentially a topological-optimisation process, but in reverse. By constraining the geometry and defining the load cases the design algorithm places material to meet the requirements. One of the most accessible generative design software packages is Fusion 360 . To put it to test, I decided to try to design a weight-optimised handlebar mount for my bike computer.

Here are some photos of the design process, generative design outcomes and the printed part installed onto the handlebar.
